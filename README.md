# 🛒 Amazon Sales Analysis

This project analyzes **Amazon sales data** using **SQL** and **Power BI** to uncover trends in sales performance, customer behavior, product lines, and revenue generation across multiple branches.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The project focuses on understanding key sales metrics from Amazon's branches. By processing the sales dataset using SQL and visualizing with Power BI, it helps drive **data-driven business decisions** through insights into product performance, customer preferences, VAT contributions, and time-based trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze **sales performance** across different product lines and branches.
- Study **customer purchasing behavior** by type, gender, and rating.
- Identify **top revenue-generating** cities and product categories.
- Understand **payment preferences** and **VAT contributions**.
- Perform **day & time-based analysis** on customer ratings and purchase behavior.

---

## 🧩 Key Features

- **📈 Sales Insights**:  
  Identify which **product lines, branches, and cities** generate the most revenue.

- **👥 Customer Analysis**:  
  Understand how different **customer types** and **genders** influence purchasing decisions and ratings.

- **💳 Payment & VAT**:  
  Analyze preferred **payment methods** and **VAT contributions** by product line and location.

- **🕒 Time-Based Trends**:  
  Explore which **hours and days** see the most purchases and highest customer ratings.

## 📊 Key Insights & Analysis

- **Best-Performing Product Lines**:  
  Identified top product lines based on **sales volume and revenue**.

- **Customer Behavior**:  
  Observed purchase trends across **gender**, **customer type**, and **purchase time**.

- **Branch & City Comparison**:  
  Ranked branches and cities by **total sales**, **ratings**, and **VAT contributions**.

- **Payment Preferences**:  
  Discovered the **most used payment methods**, aiding in transaction planning.

- **High-Rated Time Slots**:  
  Identified the **time of day** and **days of week** with the highest **customer satisfaction ratings**.
